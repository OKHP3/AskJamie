About BrandGuard

A suite of AI companions designed to watch over language, style, and moral framing — whether you’re drafting guidelines, prompts, or marketing copy.

Tone Keeper

Analyses outputs and suggests adjustments to align with your house style. Keeps cadence, vocabulary, and energy on brand.

Ethics Sentinel

Flags potentially harmful or exclusionary language and proposes inclusive alternatives. Helps you hold the line.

Framing Navigator

Guides strategic planning by mapping goals to values and stakeholder impact. Keeps long-term choices legible.

BFS Dashboard

A control centre for reviewing alignment metrics across your GPTs — tone adherence, ethical risk flags, and brand voice drift.
